Search and Rescue Robot

The final group project of "MAE 322: Mechanical Design" is to design and manufacture a Search and Rescue Robot (SaRR). I was enrolled in this course in Fall 2024. The robot traversed a course that included going over a ramp, traveling through a maze, ascending a foot high wall, and delivering a medical kit into a basket. I was responsible for coding and manufacturing the SaRR.
